Ingredients

List Of All Ingredients
Ingredient Amount needed
High-quality sandwich bread or ay bread of your choice. 2 1/2 pounds, cut into 3/4 dices
Butter of your choice 8 tabelspoons
Sausage (what I usually add to my stuffing. 11/2 pound sage sausage. How ever much you want.
Large Onion 1, (chopped well)
Large rib celery 4
Garlic cloves 2 minced cloves
Fresh minced sage leaves 1/2 cups
Kosher Salt and black pepper, if wanted. optional
Store bought turkey stock 4 cups
Large eggs 3
minced parsley leaves 1/4 cup

Instructions

These are the steps
Step Directions
1. Preheat oven to 275°F (135°C), and adjust oven racks to to respective spots, then spread two baking sheets and bake for about 50 minutes. Let cool, then increase oven temp to 350°F (180°C)
2. In a oven, melt butter over medium-high heat (for about 9 minutes). Then for the next 8 minutes add sausage, breake it up as it cooks.
3. Whisk the remaining chicken stock, eggs, and 3 tablespoons parsley in a bowl of your choice. Slowly add the egg mixture whilst stirring, and fold the bread cubes until combined.
4. When fully mixed place the remaining stuffing in a buttured baking dish of your choice (because any size is ok), and bake for 45 minutes until the center reaches 150°F (66°C). With a foil on top. After the time is done, you want to remove the foila nd bake again for 15 - 20 mins until you see a golden brown color. Then just let cool for 5 minutes ans serve.

Tips and Tricks

  1. Make sure to spice your pie, it'll add great flavor. consider cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.
  2. It is completely optional, but if you want be a little more creative and have fun with baking, try making your own Punmkin Puree, it might take a bit longer, but it'll be worth it.
  3. Overmix: something you don't want to do is overmix the ingredinets or over do the instructions, mix the filling until combined to avoid air bubbled, whihc may lead to cracking during breaking.
  4. Tempreature matters, pay attention to the tempreature you baking in the oven at, as well as the over all room tempreature your mixing ingredients at. Cold ingredients will lead to a lump filling.
  5. One trick, is to serve with passion, decorate your pie pan, add things on top such as whipping cream or, small seeds. You made this, Decorate it!
