Ingredients

List Of All Ingredients
Ingredient Amount needed
Unbaked Pie Crest 1 to 9-inches
Pure Pumpkin 1 can (15 ounces)
Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 can (14 ounces)
large eggs 2, at room tempreature
Pumpkin Pie Spice 2 teaspoon
ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon
Kosher salt 1/2 teaspoons
whipped topping of your choice optional (not needed)

Instructions

These are the steps
Steps Directions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Roll out some of the pie crust to the best of your ability, and put it in a 9-inch pie plate, cut any left out crust (maybe your can use it for later!).
3. Flute or crimp the edges (if needed) and placethe pan in the refrigerator while you prepare the filling
4. In a large bowl combine the pumpkin, the sweetened condensed milk, the eggs,the pumpkin pie spice, the cinnamon, and the salt. Then, whisk until smooth.
5. Pour the pumpkin mixture into the crust. Then Bake for 15 minutes.
6. After 15 minutes, keeping the oven door closed, reduce the temperature to 350°F and bake for 35-40 more minutes, or until a knife inserted comes out clean.
7. Let the pie get to room temperature before slicing and serving. Top with whipped cream if desired.

Tips and Tricks

  1. Make sure to spice your pie, it'll add great flavor. consider cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.
  2. It is completely optional, but if you want be a little more creative and have fun with baking, try making your own Punmkin Puree, it might take a bit longer, but it'll be worth it.
  3. Overmix: something you don't want to do is overmix the ingredinets or over do the instructions, mix the filling until combined to avoid air bubbled, whihc may lead to cracking during breaking.
  4. Tempreature matters, pay attention to the tempreature you baking in the oven at, as well as the over all room tempreature your mixing ingredients at. Cold ingredients will lead to a lump filling.
  5. One trick, is to serve with passion, decorate your pie pan, add things on top such as whipping cream or, small seeds. You made this, Decorate it!

Prep Time: This recipe should probably take about 1-1/2 hours to make, enjoy!! The servings should be about 8 generous slices.
